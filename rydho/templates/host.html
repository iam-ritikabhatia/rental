Sign up as a partner, submit your vehicle details and photos, and complete KYC. We review and list the vehicle after verification. It usually takes 2–3 working days to create the account and list your vehicle on the site.

Payments are processed and transferred to your bank within 2 working days after the booking completes.

In case of damage, repair costs are first deducted from the customer's security deposit collected at pickup. RYDHO helps facilitate the claim and communication, but repair collection is handled between the partner and the customer. We recommend partners keep a small reserve (~1% of monthly revenue) to cover any unrecoverable damage costs.

Earnings vary by model, location and availability. Depending on the bike model and condition, partners can earn up to ₹25,000 per month (and in some cases more with high-demand inventory and longer availability).

Once your account is active you will receive access to the partner app. Use the app to block specific dates for your vehicle so it won't accept bookings on those days.

If the partner cannot provide the vehicle for a confirmed booking, a penalty equal to 25% of the booking amount may be charged to compensate the customer. Repeated cancellations can affect partner standing on the platform.

RYDHO provides recommended price ranges for each model based on market data. You remain free to set your own price. Consider vehicle condition, local demand, and season when choosing your rate.

RYDHO operates as a booking platform and helps facilitate listings, bookings, and support. Partners collect a security deposit from customers for protection. While we assist you through the claims process, the partner remains responsible for the vehicle—please maintain proper insurance and consider a small reserve for unrecoverable costs.
