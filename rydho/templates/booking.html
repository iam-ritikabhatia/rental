Booking Process

How to Rent a Bike on RYDHO

We have built a frictionless, secure two-wheeler booking flow. No paperwork, no long queues. Get verified and start riding in minutes.

1

Search & Compare Rides

Select your pickup city, dates, and browse the largest catalog of local scooters and motorcycles. Compare prices and host ratings to find your perfect fit.

2

Instant Online KYC Verification

Upload a photo of your Driving License (DL) and Aadhar Card. Our digital KYC engine verifies your details in under 3 minutes, securing your booking with 0% deposit.

3

Secure Payment & Digital Agreement

Pay securely using UPI, Netbanking, or credit/debit cards. Review and digitally sign the standard rental agreement to lock in liability protections.

4

Key Handover & Return

Pick up the vehicle from the host hub or get it delivered to your doorstep. Inspect the vehicle, snap photos in the app, and start riding. Return the vehicle with the same fuel level at the end of your trip.
