Official Government RTO Fee Chart (2026)

Under Ministry of Road Transport & Highways guidelines, standard processing fees are as follows. Agents charge separate convenience fees.

RTO Service Type Government Portal Fee
Learner's License Application ₹150
Permanent DL Driving Test Fee ₹300
Duplicate Driving License Issuance ₹200
Two-Wheeler Ownership Transfer (RC) ₹150
Smart Card Registration Certificate (RC) ₹200
Hypothecation (HP) Removal on Two-Wheeler ₹500

*Please note: Some states impose local RTO cess taxes or late penalties on renewals. You can directly pay these online on the official Parivahan Sewa Portal.
