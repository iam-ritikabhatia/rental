Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking and listings.

You need a valid Driving License (DL) for two-wheelers, along with a government-approved address proof (Aadhar Card, Passport, or Voter ID). International travelers require an International Driving Permit (IDP) and a Passport.

Fuel is not included in the rental price. We provide the vehicle with a basic level of fuel to reach the nearest fuel station, and you are expected to return it with the same amount of fuel.

At RYDHO, we offer a 0% security deposit on most models for verified profiles. If a security deposit is required for premium models, it is fully refundable within 24 hours of returning the vehicle.

To list your scooter or bike, click on 'Become a Host', input your vehicle details and documents, set your price, and submit for verification. Once approved, customers in your area can book it, and you earn commissions.

Free cancellation is available up to 24 hours before your booking starts. Cancellations made within 24 hours of pickup will incur a nominal cancellation fee as detailed in our cancellation policy.
